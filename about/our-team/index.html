Qzzr’s story

In our combined past we’ve been public school teachers, corporate trainers, school-of-rock owners, learning strategists to Fortune 100 companies, coaches, creators of the world’s foremost social learning software, keynote speakers, bloggers, and Sunday school teachers.

We created Qzzr because although there are many quiz tools out there, none of them are awesome. We wanted to bring our bag of tricks to a space that has been filled with mediocre tools and create something really special.

By creating the world’s simplest, most beautiful and mobile-friendly quiz creation tool, we aim to create the world’s largest collection of quizzes on any topic accessible to anyone for free.

We like to think we’re powering the quiz revolution. Qwizards unite.

Qzzr was created by the team at Movement, the makers of  Boombox and Pollcaster.

We are a collective of talented entrepreneurs, designers, engineers, marketers, and geeks who value the lifelong relationships borne of a strong work culture.  We covet one another’s skill set.  We challenge each other to be better.  We wouldn’t do this with anyone else.

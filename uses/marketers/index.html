Qzzr for marketers

Create quizzes quickly

Qzzr makes it easy to customize your quiz, change your background, and add text and images. You could have your first quiz on your site today.

Make it an informed follow up

Once you have the lead information and their quiz responses, you can follow up in an informed way. Sideshow Collectibles used this technique to generate over $75,000 with this quiz, and this quiz from Zenni Optical has brought in over $1 million (and counting).

Export leads to your other tools

Qzzr integrates with many email marketing, marketing automation, and CRM tools to allow you to quickly follow up with your new prospects. Use data from your quizzes to get extra targeted.

Customize your form

Besides capturing emails, you can also add a custom call-to-action to send someone to a landing page. Or, you can drive new likes for your Facebook page.

Convert your visitors

Drop in a lead gen form and invite visitors to take the next step in their relationship with you. Oh, and you won't just have their email addresses -- you'll see how they responded to the questions in the quiz.

Make the cash register ring

Qzzr makes it easy for you to generate ultra-qualified leads and present targeted offers. Think of a survey, except one that people want to take, and answer honestly. And share with all their friends.

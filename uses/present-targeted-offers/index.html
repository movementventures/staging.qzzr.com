Track conversion rates

In the quiz analytics, you can measure the funnel from visitors to offers in a glance.

Get more likes

Qzzr makes growing your Facebook likes a piece of cake. Just plug in your Facebook page at the end of your quiz, share your quiz, and let Qzzr do the heavy lifting from there.

Match the offer to their answers

By putting a link in the outcome description of your quiz, you can give a special offer to someone based precisely on the answers they selected.

Customize your offer

Once you create a compelling offer and description, you can customize the button label and set the URL it directs to.

Provide an education

Alternatively to an outcome quiz, you could create a graded quiz with great feedback that teaches your visitors about your product, brand, or service. Typically our quizzes have completion rates of over 80% which means they are holding a visitor's attention for 3-5 minutes.

Help them decide

Under the hood of an outcome quiz is really a decision engine. Based on preferences, you can make a recommendation to a visitor... just like an expert would. How much more likely are your customers to buy after they speak with an expert?

Engage your visitors

Quizzes offer a low risk, high reward way for a visitor to engage with your brand. They are interactive by nature, and they give you the ability to get to know your visitors.
